HOPSTER ANNOUNCES AGREEMENT WITH COX COMMUNICATIONS


London, 16 July 2020 –Hopster, the award-winning preschool educational and entertainment digital platform that develops young children’s learning through kids TV shows, music, games and books has announced an agreement with Cox Communications.

The service launched earlier this month on 7th July 2020 and will be available to Cox Contour customers for $6.99 per month. Customers will have access to hundreds of Hopster’s educational preschool shows, video books and music videos that are built around a world wide early years curriculum.

Hopster features family-favourite classics, independent, inclusive and diverse shows from around the world. All of the content has been selected by experts and leading academics in childhood education and cognitive development.

Preschoolers will be able to enjoy fun and educational shows like science-learning Earth to Luna, numeracy-focused Numberblocks as well as family favourite shows such as Fireman Sam and Cloudbabies. Little ones will also be able entertained by Hopster Studios original content which

includes fast-paced Grimm’s Fairy Tales series Two Minute Tales, empathy-focused show Saturday Club, hygiene-focussed shorts Goodbye Germs and fun learning songs Hopster Jam. Inclusive and diverse content such as Punky, a show that is centered around a girl with down syndrome and sign-language show Signed Stories, African Nursery Rhymes can also be discovered and enjoyed. There are also lots of hugely popular video books which include Henny Penny and Little Princess, nursery rhymes, bedtime lullabies, and inspiring learning songs for every time of the day.

Nick Walters, founder and CEO at Hopster said: “We are thrilled to have partnered with Cox Communications. It’s an incredible opportunity to further develop our reach and offer millions of families in the states an opportunity to access our educational, diverse and inclusive content especially during this unprecedented time.”

About Hopster​ – www.hopster.tv

Hopster is a beautifully designed app for the under-sixes, aiming to strike the perfect balance between learning and fun. For one low monthly subscription with no tie-ins, Hopster subscribers get access to a large selection of kid’s TV shows, stimulating learning games, nursery rhymes and music.

Hopster is trusted by millions of parents worldwide to keep their kids amazed with fun educational content in a safe and ad-free environment. It has topped the App Store charts in over 36 countries, was named Best Streaming Video Platform for preschoolers by Kidscreen as has received a channel nomination at the Children’s BAFTA in 2018.

Hopster can be downloaded free from the App Store and Google Play and can be found on selected smart TVs such as Amazon Fire TV, EE TV and Roku in the UK, Roku Channels, Comcast Xfinity, DISH and Sling TV in the US and on many other platforms around the world.
