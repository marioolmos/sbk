HOPSTER PARTNERS WITH VIP MOBILE

London, 03 December 2020 –Hopster, the award-winning preschool educational and entertainment digital platform has announced that it has partnered with Vip mobile from Serbia.

The partnership which launched Monday 23rd November, offers its Vip mobile customers access to the Hopster ad-free mobile app via its direct carrier billing service that will allow its users to access the award-winning app, paying via their mobile bills.  Vip mobile Serbia customers will be able to access Hopster’s localised educational shows, music, games and books that are built around an international early years curriculum via their mobile or tablet for free from the App Store and Google Play.

Hopster offers customers hundreds of handpicked age-appropriate family favourite shows such as Pingu, Ben and Holly and Peppa Pig. Preschoolers will also be able to enjoy original animated TV series from Hopster Studios which includes science-based show Clever Brenda, fast-paced Grimm’s Fairy Tales series Two Minute Tales and empathy-focused show Saturday Club. The add-free app offers preschoolers an array of fun, engaging and educational games that develop literacy, numeracy and creativity. Hopster also features a collection of beautiful children’s ebooks, videobooks, audiobooks and favourite nursery rhymes, bedtime lullabies, and inspiring learning songs for every time of the day.

In Addition, Vip mobile customers who purchase an Alcatel 3T 8 tablet with KućniNet or Online Tariff will get Hopster free for 24 months (valid 23.11.2020 - 02.23.2021). And as a launch incentive, VIP customers with a NEO, Klasik, Klasik+, intarifa, Nova, Vip Online, Online or Kućni Nettariff will receive a free one month subscription to the Hopster mobile app (valid  23.11.2020 -  23.12.2020).

Tyrell Ellis, co-CEO at Hopster said “We are thrilled to have partnered with Vip mobile and further expand our reach in the region. With our mobile offering, preschoolers will be able to access our complete range of localised award-winning educational and fun content from our original shows to our learning games.”

Djordje Vuksanović, Senior Transformation Director of Vip mobile said “As a mobile operator we have been putting a lot of effort to provide children a safer online environment and constantly work on a better portfolio for this cause. We are very happy to partner up with Hopster and offer our customer base this great application which both parents and children will surely enjoy. As a father of two little girls, I am fully aware of the importance that screen time children spend should be more educational and of better quality .”

About Hopster​ – www.hopster.tv

Hopster is a beautifully designed app for the under-sixes, aiming to strike the perfect balance between learning and fun. For one low monthly subscription with no tie-ins, Hopster subscribers get access to a large selection of kid’s TV shows, stimulating learning games, nursery rhymes and music.

Hopster is trusted by millions of parents worldwide to keep their kids amazed with fun educational content in a safe and ad-free environment. It has topped the App Store charts in over 36 countries, was named Best Streaming Video Platform for preschoolers by Kidscreen and has received a channel nomination at the Children’s BAFTA in 2018 and 2019.

Hopster can be downloaded free from the App Store and Google Play and can be found on selected smart TVs such as Amazon Fire TV, Virgin Media and Roku in the UK, Roku Channels, Comcast Xfinity, Cox and NCTC in the US and on many other platforms around the world.

About Vip Mobile

Vip mobile is the youngest mobile operator in Serbia setting new standards on telecommunication market. Thanks to its state of the art network infrastructure, customers benefit from offers which include a wide range of cutting edge mobile communication, digital, data and IT services and solutions. In 2019 Vip achieved 25.2% market share and it is employing over 1.400 experts aiming to provide the best customer experience to more than 2.3 million customers. In 2019 the company achieved EUR 283.8 million revenues. Vip is recognized as trustful partner and a driver of positive changes in the society and environmental activism. The company is the biggest Greenfield investment in Serbia to-date having so far invested over EUR 1 billion. Vip is also the first Family Friendly company in Serbia.

Vip mobile is a member of A1 Telekom Austria Group, a leading digital services and communications provider in the CEE region with 25 million customers across seven countries, in 2019 revenues of EUR 4.57 billion, and over 18.700 employees. The Group is an European unit of América Móvil, one of the largest wireless services provider in the world.
