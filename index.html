Introducing Sandbox Kids!

Sandbox Kids is a new company that brings together the global, leading kids brands Kidomi, Hopster and Curious World.

Our mission is to endlessly engage and entertain children with content that inspires a love of learning and curiosity in the world around them. All our products are multimedia, multiplatform, 100% kid safe and can be localized for any market.

We are industry leaders with expertise in:

We have 200+ years of collective experience in digital kids content experiences with extensive expertise in designing, developing, operating and marketing premium, branded content experiences and subscription services.

Created and published over 30 kids’ mobile apps that have generated more than 100M downloads. We’re experienced in shipping kid-safe (COPPA) mobile platforms developed in UnityC++/OpenGL engine technology and Nirvana (proprietary).

We have released 9 different subscription-based products to date, including our direct-to-consumer apps Hopster, Curious World and Kidomi and enterprise platforms with Samsung, Verizon, Highlights for Children and Excelligence Learning.

We offer hand-picked, award-winning, diverse content and original exclusives for every child on a broad range of topics from STEM to social-emotional learning and age appropriate entertainment.
